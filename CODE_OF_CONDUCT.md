# Code of Conduct

VidPeek should be a useful, welcoming place to build preview-generation tools together.

## Our Standard

Please be respectful, practical, and generous with context. Good participation includes:

- Welcoming new contributors and small contributions.
- Giving feedback that is specific and actionable.
- Assuming good intent while still naming problems clearly.
- Keeping discussions focused on the project and its users.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language, sustained disruption, or publishing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block participants when behavior makes the project less safe or less productive.
